Who we are

Gimbiya Capital is a London-based entrepreneurial investment firm with a simple goal: to acquire and grow a single great business for the long term. We are not a private equity fund. We do not flip businesses. We roll up our sleeves and become true owner-operators.

what we believe

1. Businesses are built by people who care, and they should be run by people who care just as much

2. A good succession plan is more than a deal. It is about trust, respect, and carrying forward the values that made a business strong

3. Sustainable growth happens when you invest in people, processes, and the communities your business serves

Where we Focus

We look for strong, profitable businesses in the UK, especially in sectors where operational know-how and trust are everything. Our investors and advisors are experienced operators, CEOs, and entrepreneurs who share our belief in building for the long term.
